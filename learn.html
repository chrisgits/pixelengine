Site background

OCT 11, 2015

People have asked, "Chris, what in the world is that photo in the background of your site?" I say, "But of course, it is a sculpture of a famous conductor's head in Boston, MA," (think Boston Pops - Arthur Fiedler). A photo I took several years ago on a photo walk for the Photoshop User's conference there. Check out info on the sculpture here if you are interested.

I liked the way the light was creating contrast on this sculpture and emphasising the layers it is made up of. I took a few shots of the back of the sculpture where the late day sun was having fun with all the aluminum layers. The 'layers' of a website work much the same, building on each other as it is constructed. Cool stuff. Here is one of the photos I look so you can see the rest of the scupture.
